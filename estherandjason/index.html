How We Met

We were introduced through online dating in the spring of 2013. Our first date was at Ben & Jerry's Ice Cream in Memphis on free cone night.

Our Proposal

On our one year anniversary, Jason suprised me by taking me back to Ben & Jerry's for free cone night. As we got in line for ice cream, I began to notice that many of our friends were there too. I was puzzled until all of our friends began to gather around us. Jason got down on one knee, pulled out a beautiful ring, and asked me to marry him.
